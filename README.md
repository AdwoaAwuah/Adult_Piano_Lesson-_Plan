# Adult_Piano_Lesson-_Plan
 Complete 8-Week Adult Beginner Piano Lesson Plan , fully interactive and ready to use
    What each week covers:
    
    Weeks 1–2 (Foundation): Posture, note names, both clefs, LH introduction
    Week 3 (Building): First chords — C, F, G major triads and the I–IV–V progression
    Week 4 (Rhythm): Eighth notes, syncopation, groove-building
    Week 5 (Melody): Full C major scale, thumb tuck, hand position shifts
    Week 6 (Dynamics): Expression, legato/staccato, musical phrasing
    Week 7 (Performance): Polishing repertoire, mock recital, mental practice
    Week 8 (Recital): Final performance, 8-week portfolio assessment, roadmap forward
